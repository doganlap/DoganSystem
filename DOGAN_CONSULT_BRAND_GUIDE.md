# DOGAN CONSULT Brand and Content Guide

## Brand Positioning

DOGAN CONSULT positions itself as an engineering-led ICT & telecommunications consulting firm for government, utilities, and enterprise clients. Our identity emphasizes reliability, expertise, and security in mission-critical infrastructure.

**Positioning Statement:**
"For government and large enterprises needing resilient communications systems, DOGAN CONSULT is the engineering-driven consultancy that delivers secure, efficient ICT infrastructure – combining deep technical expertise with trusted project leadership."

This highlights our role as technical leaders ("engineering-driven consultancy") and the key benefits (secure, efficient infrastructure) that resonate with decision-makers seeking robust networks.

## Website Hierarchy (Site Map)

### Main Sections:
1. **Home** – Overview of capabilities, tagline, key services highlighted
2. **Services** – Detailed service categories (see below)

5. **About Us** – Company mission, leadership bios, values (engineering background, decades of experience)

## Service Offerings

### 1. Telecommunications Engineering
End-to-end network design and deployment. We plan and engineer fiber-optic, broadband and wireless networks – from strategic routing and permitting through site design and integration – to build reliable, high-speed infrastructure.

**Key Points:**
- Fiber-optic network design
- Broadband and wireless networks
- Strategic routing and permitting
- Site design and integration
- Regulatory compliance

### 2. Data Center Design
Full-lifecycle data center consulting. We provide master planning, architecture/engineering, systems integration and optimization to create resilient, efficient facilities.

**Key Points:**
- Master planning
- Architecture/engineering
- Systems integration
- Energy optimization
- Cooling and power design
- Future-ready facilities

### 3. Cybersecurity Consulting
Protecting critical infrastructure with security expertise. Services include risk and gap assessments, cybersecurity program development, governance and compliance alignment (e.g. NERC CIP, CMMC), penetration testing, threat monitoring, incident response planning, and training.

**Key Points:**
- Risk and gap assessments
- Cybersecurity program development
- Governance and compliance (NERC CIP, CMMC)
- Penetration testing
- Threat monitoring
- Incident response planning
- Security training

### 4. ICT Program & Delivery Governance
Governance and oversight to ensure project success. We help clients align IT investments with business objectives by establishing governance frameworks, strategic planning, and program management.

**Key Points:**
- IT strategic planning
- Governance frameworks
- Program management
- Project oversight
- Policy development
- Resource allocation

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary Colors**: Deep blues (#003366, #004080) and teals (#008080, #006666)
- **Neutral Colors**: Grays for stability and professionalism
- **Accent Colors**: Gold or orange sparingly for innovation/leadership

### Typography
- **Headings**: Modern sans-serif (Open Sans or Montserrat)
- **Body Text**: Clean, legible fonts (Source Serif or similar)
- **Style**: Professional, easy-to-read, consistent

### Tone & Imagery
- High-tech and executive environments
- Engineers at work, modern infrastructure
- Network schematics, diagrams
- Professional, confident, secure visual style

### Brand Voice
- Formal, authoritative voice
- Problem-solving focus
- Industry-specific terminology (NERC CIP, CMMC, SCADA, mission-critical)
- Demonstrates deep domain knowledge
- Highlights how we solve specific challenges

## Market Fit and Buyer Personas

### Target Sectors
1. **Government & Public Sector**: Federal, state, and local agencies (transportation, defense, public safety)
2. **Telecommunications Companies**: Carriers and network operators deploying fiber, 5G/IoT infrastructure
3. **Utilities & Energy**: Electric, water, and gas utilities with SCADA and smart grid systems
4. **Critical Infrastructure Operators**: Transport (rail, airports), data centers, industrial facilities

### Buyer Personas
1. **Chief Information Officer / CTO**: Focused on innovation, digital transformation, sustainability
2. **ICT Program/Project Lead**: Manages large-scale IT or telecom programs, cares about governance and outcomes
3. **Engineering/Network Manager**: Oversees technical implementation, values detailed engineering expertise
4. **Procurement/Contracts Officer**: Ensures vendor qualifications, certifications, and fair contracting
